How to set SMART goals:

Specific
State exactly what you want to achieve. Can you break a larger task down into smaller items?
Measureable
Establish clear definitions to help you measure if you're reaching your goal.
Attainable
Describe your goals using action verbs and outline the exact steps you will take to accomplish your goal.
Realistic
Give yourself the opportunity to succeed by setting goals you'll actually be able to accomplish. Be sure to consider obstacles you may need to overcome.
Timely
How much time do you have to complete the task? Decide exactly when you'll start and finish your goal.
